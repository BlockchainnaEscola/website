Projects

Our key initiatives transforming Web3 education across Latin America.

EduLatam

Comic book-based Web3 curriculum designed for accessibility across different education levels. Launched in 2025 with 500+ people trained in the first week alone, demonstrating the scalability of engaging educational materials.

Impact: 850+ trained in 2 months | Scalable curriculum model

Workshop Mao na Massa (Hands-On Workshop)

Intensive 7-day bootcamp in partnership with 42 Rio. Trained 10 programming students with zero blockchain knowledge who went on to win 2nd place at the Modular Carnival hackathon and secured a development project with Scroll.

Impact: 2nd place hackathon | Scroll project secured | Zero to job-ready in 7 days

BH Crypto Day

First cryptocurrency event in Belo Horizonte history, organized in 2024. Brought together students, developers, and Web3 enthusiasts for a day of learning and networking.

Impact: 100 participants | First crypto event in BH | Community building milestone

Web3Day Salvador

Multi-protocol educational event in Salvador featuring representatives from 6 different blockchain protocols. Provided students exposure to the breadth of the Web3 ecosystem.

Impact: 80 participants | 6 protocols represented | Cross-chain education

Modular Carnival

Massive youth-focused Web3 event in February 2025, co-organized with Cola BH and Modular. Set records for youth turnout at a Web3 event in Belo Horizonte with students from multiple educational institutions.

Impact: 1,200+ registrations | 800 in-person attendees | Largest youth Web3 event in BH

Chapada Diamantina Expansion

Launched the first blockchain club in Chapada Diamantina in 2025, partnering with IFBA Seabra to bring Web3 education to one of Brazil's most remote and scenic regions.

Impact: 250 participants | First blockchain club in region | Rural expansion model

Global Pizza Party BH

Part of the worldwide PizzaDAO celebration commemorating Bitcoin Pizza Day. Organized the Belo Horizonte edition bringing together the local crypto community for networking and education.

Impact: 200+ participants in BH | Global community connection | Cultural celebration

On-Chain Certification System

Built the largest blockchain education certification system in Latin American public schools. All certificates are verifiable on-chain, providing students with immutable proof of their learning.

Impact: 34,000+ certifications | Verifiable credentials | Industry-recognized

Teacher Training Program

Capacity building program to train public school teachers in blockchain fundamentals, enabling them to continue educating students independently.

Impact: 20+ teachers trained | Sustainable education model | Multiplier effect

Celo/Valora Onboarding

Hands-on onboarding program getting students set up with their first crypto wallets on Celo using the Valora app, providing practical experience with Web3 tools.

Impact: 1,000+ onboarded in 2025 | Real wallet experience | Financial inclusion
