How We Work

5 States 6+ Cities

Distributed & Decentralized

BnE operates across 5 Brazilian states with team members in 6+ cities:

  • Espírito Santo: Serra, Vila Velha
  • Minas Gerais: Belo Horizonte
  • São Paulo: São Paulo, Campinas
  • Bahia: Salvador, Lauro de Freitas

We coordinate remotely using Web3 tools (DAO governance, on-chain proposals) and meet in person for major events and strategic planning.

DAO On-chain

DAO Governance

Major decisions require DAO member approval:

  • Budget allocations for new programs
  • Strategic partnerships (protocols, institutions)
  • Geographic expansion plans
  • Curriculum development and changes
  • Event organization and investments

Proposals are submitted on-chain, discussed by DAO members, and voted on transparently.

Flexible

Roles Are Fluid

Team members wear multiple hats. A partnership lead might teach workshops. An operational coordinator might write articles. A designer might manage social media. This flexibility lets us move fast, adapt to opportunities, and maximize impact with a lean team.

Mission-Driven

Geographic Diversity Drives Our Mission

Our team's geographic spread isn't accidental—it's core to our mission:

  • Major tech hubs (São Paulo, Belo Horizonte): Access to institutional partners, protocols, and resources
  • Regional capitals (Salvador, Vila Velha): Bridge between major cities and interior regions
  • Small/medium cities (Serra 500k): Proof our model works beyond major metros
  • Remote region partnerships (Chapada Diamantina): Extending Ethereum to indigenous communities

This distribution ensures our education model works everywhere, not just wealthy urban centers.
