How We Work

6 States 15+ Cities

Distributed Across Brazil

We operate across 6 Brazilian states with team members in 15+ cities:

  • Espírito Santo: Serra, Vila Velha, Vitória, João Neiva, Colatina, Viana, Fundão
  • Minas Gerais: Belo Horizonte, Ouro Preto
  • São Paulo: São Paulo, Campinas
  • Bahia: Salvador, Lauro de Freitas, Cruz das Almas, Seabra, Lençóis, Vale do Capão
  • Rio de Janeiro: Rio de Janeiro
  • Pernambuco: Porto de Suape

We coordinate remotely and meet in person for major events and strategic planning.

Simple Transparent

Simple & Transparent Governance

We keep governance straightforward and simple:

  • One Telegram group for coordination and decisions
  • Multisig wallet for transparency in fund management
  • One Charmverse for money allocation and budget proposals

This lean structure lets us move fast while maintaining accountability.

Part of Refaz Brasil

UNESCO Partner

Blockchain na Escola operates as a program of Refaz Brasil, a UNESCO Partner (SoST - Transcriativa) focused on regenerative finance and local action. This partnership provides organizational structure, institutional legitimacy, and connection to global ReFi ecosystem.
