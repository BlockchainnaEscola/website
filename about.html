About

Mission

Democratizing access to blockchain and Web3 technologies in Latin America by providing free, high-quality education to public schools and underserved communities.

History

2022

First experimental program in Serra, ES; proof-of-concept validated as the first blockchain program in Brazilian public schools.

2023

Expansion to 4 states, 19 workshops in Salvador, 400+ students reached, 20 teachers trained in blockchain fundamentals.

2024

BH Crypto Day launched (first crypto event in Belo Horizonte), DevCon Bangkok presentation, Rio On Chain participation, 30,000+ certifications issued.

2025

EduLatam launch (500 trained in week 1), Modular Carnival (1,200 registrations), Chapada Diamantina expansion (250 participants), plans to expand to 27 cities in Bahia.

Core Competencies

Mass Student Mobilization

Proven ability to engage and mobilize large numbers of students for Web3 education.

Evidence: 1,200+ registrations for a single event (Modular Carnival 2025).

Rapid Skill Development

Intensive training methodology that produces tangible results quickly.

Evidence: 10 students went from zero blockchain knowledge to hackathon winners in just 7 days.

Geographic Expansion & Replication

Scalable model that can be replicated across different regions and contexts.

Evidence: Expanded from 4 states to planning 27 cities in Bahia alone.

On-Chain Credentialing at Scale

Infrastructure for issuing and verifying blockchain-based educational certificates.

Evidence: 34,000+ verifiable on-chain certifications issued.

Accessible Education Methodology

Curriculum designed for accessibility across different backgrounds and education levels.

Evidence: Comic book curriculum, culturally relevant approach that resonates with diverse communities.

Multi-Sector Partnership Building

Ability to forge and maintain partnerships across education, technology, and social sectors.

Evidence: 44+ active partnerships including universities, protocols, and community organizations.
