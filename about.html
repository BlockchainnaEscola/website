Mission

Democratizing access to blockchain and Web3 technologies in Latin America by providing free, high-quality education to public schools and underserved communities.

History

2022

First experimental program in Serra, ES; proof-of-concept validated as the first blockchain program in Brazilian public schools.

2023

Expansion to 4 states, 19 workshops in Salvador, 400+ students reached, 20 teachers trained in blockchain fundamentals.

2024

BH Crypto Day launched (first crypto event in Belo Horizonte), DevCon Bangkok presentation, Rio On Chain participation, 30,000+ certifications issued.

2025

EduLatam launch (500 trained in week 1), Modular Carnival (1,200 registrations), Chapada Diamantina expansion (250 participants), plans to expand to 27 cities in Bahia.
