Mission

Democratizing access to blockchain and Web3 technologies in Latin America by providing free, high-quality education to public schools and underserved communities.

History

2022: The Beginning

  • First experimental program in Serra, ES (February 2022)
  • Validated methodology for teaching Web3 in underserved communities
  • First blockchain program built according to Brazilian public school legal framework
  • Partnership with 101.xyz for on-chain certifications

2023: First Expansion

  • Expansion to Minas Gerais and Bahia
  • First partnerships in Rio de Janeiro
  • 19 workshops in Salvador alone
  • 400+ students and 20 teachers trained in first semester
  • 600+ people impacted by year end

2024: National Presence

  • Expansion to Rio de Janeiro and São Paulo
  • Multiple events across Bahia
  • BH Crypto Day - first crypto event in Belo Horizonte (August)
  • DevCon Bangkok presentation (November)
  • Peak of 30,000+ certifications issued at 101.xyz

2025: Rural Expansion & Scale

  • EduLatam: 840 certificates minted in 3 weeks
  • 1,200 students mobilized in 1 month
  • Expansion to rural areas
  • First Web3 event in Seabra - Chapada Diamantina region
  • First Web3 event in Recôncavo Baiano - Cruz das Almas
  • Expansion to Pernambuco (Porto de Suape)
