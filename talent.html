Connecting Brazilian university students to Ethereum careers through structured certification, portfolio building, and direct company matching.

The Challenge

Brazil is the 6th largest crypto market globally, yet lacks structured pathways for youth entering Web3 careers.

  • 52% increase in blockchain skills demand since 2022
  • Universities lack Web3 curriculum and career guidance
  • Companies struggle to find qualified local talent
  • Students don't know where to start or how to build portfolios

The Solution

Platform

Integrated Talent Pipeline

An integrated platform combining Airtable, Web3 authentication, and AI-powered matching to connect students with opportunities:

  • 6 certification tracks from fundamentals to specialized skills
  • Automated matching between student profiles and company needs
  • Hands-on portfolio building through real projects
  • Direct connection to companies, hackathons, and bounties
