Connecting Brazilian university students to Web3 careers across the EVM ecosystem, regenerative finance, and circular economy initiatives.

The Challenge

Brazil is the 6th largest crypto market globally, yet lacks structured pathways for youth entering Web3 careers.

  • 52% increase in blockchain skills demand since 2022
  • Universities lack Web3 curriculum and career guidance
  • Companies struggle to find qualified local talent
  • Students don't know where to start or how to build portfolios
  • ReFi and circular economy projects need local builders who understand community needs

The Solution

Platform

Integrated Talent Pipeline

An integrated platform combining Airtable, Web3 authentication, and AI-powered matching to connect students with opportunities across the EVM ecosystem:

  • 6 certification tracks from fundamentals to specialized skills
  • Automated matching between student profiles and company needs
  • Hands-on portfolio building through real projects
  • Direct connection to companies, hackathons, bounties, and ReFi initiatives

Regenerative Finance & Circular Economy

ReFi Circular Economy

Building for Regeneration

We're not just training developers—we're preparing talent for a regenerative economy. Our curriculum integrates:

  • Regenerative Finance (ReFi) - Using Web3 to create positive environmental and social impact
  • Circular Economy - Designing systems where value flows back to communities
  • Community Currencies - Local tokens that strengthen regional economies
UBI Experiments

Universal Basic Income Experiments

Students will learn from and contribute to real UBI experiments happening across Brazil and Latin America:

  • Understanding tokenized UBI distribution mechanisms
  • Analyzing impact data from existing pilots
  • Designing sustainable funding models through circular economy principles
  • Building tools that connect UBI recipients to local value networks

UBI isn't just about distribution—it's about creating interconnected local economies where value circulates and multiplies within communities.
